Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Introduction

FitnessPoker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

The simple truth: FitnessPoker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your workout data stays on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

FitnessPoker is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any other personal information

How FitnessPoker Works

FitnessPoker operates entirely offline on your device. All game data, including:

  • Player names and configurations
  • Exercise customizations
  • Game statistics and history
  • Timer settings and preferences

Are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server or third party.

Data Storage

Any data created within FitnessPoker (such as custom exercises or game settings) is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by our app
  • May be included in your device's standard iCloud backup if you have that enabled

Third-Party Services

FitnessPoker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics service
  • Facebook SDK or social media integrations
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any other third-party SDKs

Children's Privacy

FitnessPoker is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special provisions needed for children under 13. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Since FitnessPoker doesn't collect user data, we cannot notify you directly of changes, so we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information for you to access, correct, or delete. You have complete control over your data as it never leaves your device.

Summary

Your privacy is absolute with FitnessPoker:

  • ✓ No data collection

  • ✓ No ads
